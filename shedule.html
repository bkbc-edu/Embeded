Hackathon Schedule

9:00 AM

Welcome & Inauguration

Kick off the event with a warm welcome from the organizers and an overview of the hackathon.

10:00 AM

Idea Submission

Teams brainstorm and submit their project ideas for review and approval.

11:00 AM

Start Coding

The coding marathon begins. Participants start developing their clones and enhancements.

1:00 PM

Lunch Break

Enjoy a refreshing lunch break and recharge for the second half of the day.

3:00 PM

Mentor Check-In

Mentors visit teams to provide guidance, feedback, and technical support.

6:00 PM

Progress Presentation

Teams present their progress and receive constructive feedback from judges.

8:00 PM

Dinner Break

Relax and refuel with a delicious dinner before continuing the hackathon.

12:00 AM

Midnight Coding Challenge

An optional fun challenge to keep the energy high and the creativity flowing.

8:00 AM

Breakfast

Start the day with a healthy breakfast to prepare for the final stretch.

10:00 AM

Submission Deadline

All teams must upload their projects to GitHub and submit the link for evaluation.

11:00 AM

Final Presentations

Teams present their projects to the judges, highlighting features, innovations, and learnings.

1:00 PM

Closing Ceremony & Awards

Winners are announced, and awards are distributed. Thank you for participating!
