About the Hackathon

Organized by B. K. Birla College, Kalyan, this hackathon aims to foster innovation in web development by challenging participants to clone and enhance popular service-based websites.

Participants will create and enhance clones of famous service-based websites like IKEA, Domino's, Zepto, Amazon, etc., adding creative animations, backend functionalities, and presenting a working prototype.
