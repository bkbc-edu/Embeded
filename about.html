Welcome to "Embedded by BK Birla" Hackathon

Unleash your creativity and technical skills at "Embedded by BK Birla," a one-of-a-kind hackathon organized by B. K. Birla College, Kalyan. This event is designed to challenge developers, designers, and tech enthusiasts to push boundaries and deliver innovative solutions in the realm of service-based website development.

Event Theme: Clone, Enhance, Innovate

The hackathon revolves around reimagining some of the world's most popular service-based platforms. Participants will:

  • Clone: Create a replica of existing websites such as IKEA, Domino's, Zepto, or Amazon.
  • Enhance: Add a unique touch by integrating modern animations, intuitive user interfaces, and advanced functionalities.
  • Innovate: Implement a working backend to demonstrate seamless data handling and functionality, resulting in a fully operational prototype.

Additional Requirements

  • Code Submission: Each team must upload their completed project to a GitHub repository and provide access to the judging panel.
  • Final Presentation: Teams will present their website demos live in front of a panel of judges, showcasing:
    • The website's features.
    • Innovations and enhancements made.
    • Backend functionality and user experience.

Why Participate?

  • Skill Enhancement: Sharpen your web development, UI/UX design, and backend integration skills.
  • Networking Opportunities: Collaborate with like-minded peers, industry experts, and mentors.
  • Recognition: Stand out with your creative solutions and receive accolades from a distinguished panel of judges.
  • Attractive Prizes:
    • 1st Prize: ₹4000
    • 2nd Prize: ₹1500
    • 3rd Prize: ₹1000

What to Expect?

  • Collaborative Environment: Work in teams to tackle exciting challenges.
  • Hands-On Development: From ideation to execution, create a project that reflects your expertise and creativity.
  • Support and Guidance: Mentors and industry professionals will be available to provide feedback and technical support.

Our Mission

"Embedded by BK Birla" aims to inspire innovation and excellence in web development by providing a platform for participants to showcase their talents. This event reflects our commitment to nurturing the next generation of tech leaders and fostering a culture of creativity and collaboration.
